Recommendation: mark + mascot, not one or the other

The friendly mascot (08) is lovely but a touch detailed for a 1:1 app tile. The clean move: keep Honeycomb cell as the primary logo / app icon (it’s already wired into the app), and use the flat friendly bee as a mascot — onboarding heroes, empty states, the voice-recording moment, stickers. Best of both: a crisp brand mark and a warm character that shows up when it counts.
